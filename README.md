# Timecasters Strategy

Working notes and research on product strategy for Timecasters / AstroPrompt — product-market fit, go-to-market channels, and the longer-term architectural direction (question classifier + MCP servers for astrological calculations).

## Layout

- `notes/` — stream-of-consciousness and synthesized strategy notes
- `research/` — market, competitor, and technical research
- `interviews/` — notes from conversations with astrologers, users, and advisors
- `validation/` — experiments and evidence for/against hypotheses
- `decisions/` — decision records (what was chosen and why)
